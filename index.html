Personal interests

I have always loved learning. I never thought that I would major in software engineering, but the moment I realized that it was an option, I knew it was the perfect fit for me. Studying to be a software engineer has been one of the most rewarding learning experiences of my life!

One of my biggest hobbies is playing videogames. I have loved them ever since I was a little girl and saw my brother playing The Elder Scrolls: Oblivion. I love playing all genres of videogames, but some of my favorite genres are RPG's, FPS's, and Survival Horror. My favorite videogame series has to be the Soulsborne games. I am proud to say that I have beaten all of them!

I love traveling and experiencing new things! One goal that I have is to travel to every continent in the world. My favorite thing to do when I'm somewhere new is to try the local restaurants.
